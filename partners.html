Partner With RILOC

Real impact happens across sectors. Join a coalition that turns partnership into measurable community change.

Partnership Tracks

Ways your organization can partner

NGOs & Nonprofits

Co-deliver programs, share referral networks, and align advocacy efforts across the coalition.

Businesses

Sponsor programs, host internships and hiring pipelines, or provide in-kind resources and expertise.

Government Agencies

Coordinate on resettlement services, public health, and policy initiatives that affect our shared community.

Universities

Partner on research, offer scholarships, and connect students to service-learning opportunities.

Community Organizations

Cross-refer members, co-host events, and pool resources for greater collective reach.

Foundations & Funders

Invest in coordinated, community-led programs with transparent reporting and measurable outcomes.
