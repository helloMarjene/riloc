Volunteer With Us

From tutoring to translation, your time creates real momentum for families building a new home.

Opportunities

Ways to get involved

Tutoring & Mentorship

Support youth and adult learners in academics, ESL, and career mentorship. 2–4 hrs/week.

Interpretation & Translation

Help bridge language gaps at appointments, workshops, and events. Flexible scheduling.

Event & Logistics Support

Assist with community events, donation drives, and program setup. One-time or ongoing.

Housing Move-In Teams

Help set up new apartments for arriving families with furniture and essentials.

Digital Literacy Coaching

Guide participants through computer basics, job portals, and online tools.

Professional Skill-Sharing

Lead a workshop in your area of expertise — finance, legal, design, or trades.

FAQ

Volunteer Questions

Do I need to speak a second language?

No — many roles don't require it. Multilingual volunteers are especially valued for interpretation roles.

What is the minimum time commitment?

Most roles ask for 2–4 hours weekly for at least one semester, though one-time event volunteers are always welcome.

Is training provided?

Yes. All volunteers complete a short orientation, and youth-facing roles include additional safety training.
