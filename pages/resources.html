Questions

About Our Resources

Can't find what you need? Contact our team and we'll help.

Are these resources free to download?

Yes. All RILOC resources are freely available to community members, partner organizations, and the public. Some partner-only toolkits require a simple registration.

Can I share these resources with my organization?

Absolutely. We encourage sharing. Most resources are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial. Please credit RILOC when distributing.

Do you offer resources in languages other than English?

Many of our core guides are available in Spanish, Arabic, and Somali. We're actively expanding translations based on community need.

How can I request a printed copy?

Contact our Community Outreach department at hello@riloc.org or visit any RILOC center. Printed copies are available at no cost while supplies last.
