Our Departments

Eight specialized teams, one coordinated mission — each department owns a piece of the pathway to belonging.

Department

Community Outreach

The front door of RILOC — connecting newly arrived families to services, information, and a welcoming first contact.

Department

Youth & Education

Runs ESL, GED, tutoring, mentorship, and college-prep programs that keep young people and adult learners on track.

Department

Workforce Development

Builds career pathways through readiness training, coaching, internships, and industry certification programs.

Department

Health & Wellness

Delivers mental health support, community wellness programming, and equitable healthcare navigation.

Department

Advocacy & Civic Engagement

Leads policy advocacy, civic education, and human-rights programming that protects community interests.

Department

Partnerships

Cultivates relationships with NGOs, businesses, government, and universities to expand coalition capacity.

Department

Women & Family Empowerment

Focuses on women's wellness, family support, and household stability programming.

Department

Research & Policy

Tracks outcomes, publishes findings, and informs coalition strategy and public policy positions with evidence.
