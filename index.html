Our Mission

Empowering communities to belong, lead, and thrive.

RILOC is a nonprofit community consortium dedicated to empowering refugees, immigrants, and underserved populations through economic empowerment, advocacy, leadership development, and community integration.

Our vision is to create thriving, inclusive, and economically empowered communities where refugees and immigrants achieve long-term success.

Core Values

What guides every program we build

Inclusion

We embrace cultural diversity and promote equal opportunities for all individuals and families.

Empowerment

We equip communities with education, leadership skills, and economic opportunities.

Collaboration

We foster partnerships among nonprofits, government agencies, businesses, and community leaders.

Integrity

We maintain transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership in all operations.

Service

We are committed to compassionate, community-focused support and advocacy.

Sustainability

We promote long-term solutions that strengthen community resilience and independence.

What We Do

Programs making a difference every day

From the first day of arrival to long-term leadership, we walk alongside every family.

Housing

Housing Assistance

Direct support finding safe, affordable housing, understanding lease agreements, and connecting to rental assistance resources. 90% of families remain stably housed one year later.

1,200+ families Avg. 45 days
Education

Digital Literacy

Hands-on training in computer basics, internet safety, email, and essential workplace software. Loaner devices available during training.

2,000+ graduates 8-week course
Health

Mental Health Support

Culturally responsive counseling and peer-support groups addressing trauma, displacement stress, and adjustment challenges in multiple languages.

500+ sessions 6 languages
Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship Program

Business planning, micro-lending guidance, and mentorship for aspiring immigrant and refugee entrepreneurs launching local businesses.

150+ businesses Micro-loans
Leadership

Leadership Development

Cohort-based program preparing community members for board seats, civic roles, and organizational leadership positions.

100 leaders/year Certified
Advocacy

Policy Advocacy

Coalition-led advocacy at the local and state level to protect and advance refugee and immigrant rights through community voices.

12 policy wins Active
Why RILOC

Led by the communities we serve — not just for them

RILOC exists because refugee and immigrant communities know their own needs best. We are a coalition built and staffed by people with lived experience of displacement and resettlement.

Community-Led

Governed and guided by refugee and immigrant leaders.

Proven Outcomes

Measured results across housing, jobs, and wellbeing.

Wide Reach

Coalition partners spanning eight core departments.

Transparent

Open governance and published annual reporting.
